Game Storyline

Water Mission 2.0 is an adventure game, where a scuba diver has to collect 20 rubies while avoiding sea enemies like octopuses, seahorses and sharks. To complete the mission the scuba diver has to collect all 20 rubies. The player accumulates 5 points for each ruby collected.

Game Instructions

How to play?
Use arrow keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) to control the player Player earns 5 points for each ruby.
